# 👥 KeenKeeper — Friendship Analytics & Tracking

KeenKeeper is a professional relationship management tool designed to help users nurture and maintain their most meaningful connections through data-driven insights and interaction logging.

### 🛠️ Technologies Used
* **React.js** with **React Router DOM** for seamless navigation.
* **Tailwind CSS** & **daisyUI** for a responsive, modern "Mobile-First" UI.
* **Recharts** for interactive friendship analytics.
* **Vite** for a high-performance development environment.

### 🚀 Key Features
1. **Dynamic Interaction Logging:** Log calls, texts, and videos with a single click, automatically updating your history.
2. **Friendship Analytics:** A visual breakdown of your communication habits using interactive donut charts.
3. **Smart Timeline:** A filtered history of all your past check-ins to ensure no one gets left behind.
